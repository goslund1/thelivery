## Save screen UX (flagged, not yet fixed)

Several confirm/save interactions grey out a tall card or modal and center the panel in the middle of that greyed space — not the center of the viewport. User has to scroll to find the interaction panel.

**Root cause:** overlays using `position: absolute; inset: 0; align-items: center; justify-content: center` relative to a tall parent instead of `position: fixed`. Confirmed in `NewCardModal.vue`: `.nc-folder-prompt` (line ~492) is `position: absolute` inside `.nc-modal-card`. When the modal card is taller than the viewport, the centered panel lands off-screen.

**Instances to audit:**
- `NewCardModal.vue` — `.nc-folder-prompt` (folder-name prompt)
- Check `EditCardModal.vue` for any inline confirm overlays
- Check any `RecipeSection` or `TuningAdjustments` save/confirm dialogs

**Fix direction:** change problem overlays to `position: fixed; inset: 0` so they anchor to the viewport. Alternatively, promote the save action to a proper fixed footer strip or floating bar (better UX, avoids the dimming pattern entirely for quick saves).
